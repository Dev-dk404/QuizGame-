Hi, I am Devendra Adhikari. I am first year student currently studying in Griffith College, Cork. I created this quiz as part of my assignment. The features of this quiz are listed below:

  1. Displays one question at a time.
  2. Right and wrong answers can be found through colour indicator
  3. Displays 5 random questions from the bank of 10 questions every time you play.
  4. Two question types: multiple choice radio buttons and image options.
  5. Renders score in percentage at the very end of the quiz.
